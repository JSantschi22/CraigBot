# CraigBot
A llama-index and Groq powered RAG chatbot for the official USA ultimate ruleset and relevant MODS amendments.
A personal project developed for my rec ultimate team and my fellow high school ultimate coaches. CraigBot is 
additionally trained on a series of strategy presentations usually used for coaching.

The name CraigBot comes from beloved player and coach, Craig Simpson. 

Rules: https://usaultimate.org/rules/
Amendments: https://mods.mb.ca/about/rules/

Live on craigbot.ca
